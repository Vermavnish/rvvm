About Our School

Our Legacy

Governed by: Shri Vindhyachal Seva Samiti

Founder: Lt. Vindhyachal Prasad Singh (1924-2009)

Chairman: Mr. Ravi Shankar Singh

Director: Mr. Bhaskar Singh

Radhika Vilas Vidya Mandir has been a beacon of quality education in Chakia, Ballia since 1993. We provide a nurturing environment where students develop academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our school offers education from Nursery to Class 10th with both English Medium (Nursery-8th) and Hindi Medium (9th-10th) affiliated to U.P. Board.

Location: SH-1B, Chakia (Dalpatpur), Ballia, Uttar Pradesh
